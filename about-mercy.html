Mercy's Biography

Early years

Mercy Ebere Njoku, popularly known as Splendor was born in Plateau State, Nigeria to Mr.and Mrs. Simon and Christiana Njoku, on the 23rd of September, 1985. She attended COCIN Salvation Nuresery & Primary School, Shendam, Plateau State. After her primary school education, she moved to Lagos where she stayed with her older cousin and family to further her secondary education.

While there, she experienced so much difficulty but persevered.
